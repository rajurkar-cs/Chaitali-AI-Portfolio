DALL·E – Teaching AI to Imagine

A group project exploring generative AI evolution and ethics

Overview

This artifact summarizes a collaborative project exploring OpenAI’s DALL·E model. It includes a historical timeline, industry applications, examples of both success and failure, and predictions for the future of generative AI in visual domains.

Objective

To examine the evolution of DALL·E, identify key use cases across industries, and analyze ethical challenges related to bias, misinterpretation, and deepfake risks.

Key Highlights

  • Timeline: From DALL·E 1 (2021) to DALL·E 3 (2024) with integration into ChatGPT.
  • Use Cases: Education, marketing, design, healthcare, and publishing.
  • Failures: Stereotypical outputs, scientific inaccuracies, poor anatomy rendering.
  • Predictions: Text-to-video generation, embedded use in apps like Canva and PowerPoint.

Tools & Technologies

  • PowerPoint
  • DALL·E (OpenAI)
  • AI prompt testing (illustrative examples)

Value Proposition

This artifact shows the ability to critically assess AI tools, communicate their implications, and balance excitement for innovation with ethical considerations. It emphasizes group collaboration and visual storytelling.
